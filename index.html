BMI Calculator

The BMI Calculator determines body fat based on weight and height, providing a quick health assessment and fitness level indication.

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy

Calculate your BMI

To calculate your BMI, divide your weight (kg) by your height (m) squared. It indicates body fat and health risk.
